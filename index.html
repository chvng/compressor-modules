Compressor modules


ABB Gas Compressor Modules help oil and gas producers reduce implementation time and labor by delivering a fully designed, assembled and commissioned solution.

Being the world's first digital gas compressor modules, these solutions provide operators with real-time insights about compressor status to ensure more precise management and maintenance.

Benefits

Modular mobile solutions

  • Accelerate delivery, optimize performance and reduce costs.
  • Reduce risk with fully designed, pre-tested, validated, assembled and commissioned solutions.
  • Easily relocate when wells become depleted or not economically efficient.
  • Integrate seamlessly with other ABB modules (e.g. e-houses, integrated control and safety systems, metering modules, portable transformers and sub-stations.

Digital compressor modules

  • Enable easy monitoring and management to improve efficiency and preserve uptime.
  • Provide operational insights to support predictive maintenance routines and reduce total cost of ownership.

A wide range of efficient drivers

  • Enable more predictable and efficient operations
  • Further increase mobility and enable remote deployment with gas-fueled options
  • Provide a green and efficient alternative, using our electrical drive trains (with both fixed and variable-speed options), wherever power sources are available
